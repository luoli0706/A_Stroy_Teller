# Default Story Framework

## Genre
- Mystery adventure

## Structural Constraints
- Three-act structure
- Every act must contain one clue revelation
- Final act must include a verifiable resolution

## Narrative Constraints
- Keep timeline explicit
- Preserve role perspective differences without breaking shared facts
- Keep tone suitable for teen and young adult readers

## Output Targets
- 6-10 paragraphs for integrated story
- Include a compact title
