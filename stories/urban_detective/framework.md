# Urban Detective Framework

## Genre
- Modern city detective drama

## Structural Constraints
- Act 1: Incident and first clue
- Act 2: Contradictions and suspect network
- Act 3: Deduction, confrontation, and proof

## Narrative Constraints
- Emphasize evidence chain and witness credibility
- Keep role viewpoints complementary but not duplicated
- Avoid supernatural explanations

## Role Slots
- **Lead Detective**: A sharp, perhaps cynical investigator. Needs strong deduction skills and a distinct observation style.
- **Supportive Partner/Informant**: Provides technical support or street-level intelligence. Often acts as a moral compass or a risk-taker.
- **Key Suspect/Shadow**: A complex figure with a hidden agenda. Needs to be evasive yet compelling.

## Output Targets
- Include a case codename in title
- End with a clear truth statement
